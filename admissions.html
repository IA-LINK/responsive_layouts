Academic Programs

We offer a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various faculties, including:

  • Faculty of Education
  • Faculty of Science and Technology
  • Faculty of Arts and Social Sciences
  • Faculty of Vocational and Technical Education

Admissions Process

Admissions are open to qualified candidates who meet the academic and ethical standards of the university. Steps include:

  • Application through JAMB or Direct Entry
  • University Post-UTME Screening
  • Verification of Credentials
  • Payment of Acceptance Fees
